About DealOrbit

DealOrbit is a comprehensive rotation management system designed specifically for automotive dealerships to ensure fair and equitable distribution of finance deals among F&I managers.

Our Mission

We believe in fairness, transparency, and efficiency. DealOrbit eliminates the guesswork and manual tracking that often leads to unfair deal distribution. Our system ensures every F&I manager gets equal opportunities, building trust and improving team morale.

Why DealOrbit?

Traditional methods of deal distribution often rely on memory, favoritism, or manual tracking - all of which can lead to errors and disputes. DealOrbit automates the entire process, ensuring fairness while saving time and reducing conflicts.

Key Benefits

  • Fairness: Every manager gets equal opportunities
  • Transparency: Complete visibility into the rotation system
  • Efficiency: Automated assignment eliminates manual work
  • Accountability: Track every deal with full audit trails
  • Simplicity: Easy to use, no training required
